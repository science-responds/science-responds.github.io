Funding and Project Support Opportunities

We list here a number of funding opportunities related to COVID-19 reseearch and activities to fight the pandemic and its effects. Opportunities related to computing resources (e.g. HPC or HTC allocations) are listed separately on Computing Resources page.

  • US National Science Foundation (NSF)
    • Dear Colleague Letter on the Coronavirus Disease 2019 (COVID-19)
  • Creative Destruction Lab (CDL)
    • The Creative Destruction Lab is a non-profit organization that delivers programs for seed-stage science- and technology-based companies. The have setup a recovery program specifically for products and services that public health and economic challenges posed by COVID-19. The program will be global and online, with an accelerated schedule as compared to the usual program.
  • National Sciences and Engineering Resource Council of Canada (NSERC)
    • NSERC Alliance COVID-19 Grants are being made available to projects related to COVID-19 research in Canada.
